About

Welcome to the Leftist News Network! The purpose of this site is to act as a centralized informational hub for left independent media. You will find information and links for various YouTube, Podcasts, Twitch and magazine content creators. These individuals and ogranizations are all growing in popularity but we have still not made it to the level of changing the mainstream media narrative. The left needs to band together to make this work. My goal is to help commit to this in the long run.

For the newcomers who stumble upon this site and are curious. The left represents policy positions such as Medicare for All, Green New Deal, College for all, LGTBQ+ equality, establishing unions and changing the gross inequalities of wealth and power. These are just a few of the many policies the left stands for. We represent the needs of the many and not the few. Check out any one of the content creators on this site. All offer various scenarios and tactics in which the left can succeed. We are all in this together. Let's fight together.

If you're able take the time to donate to these amazing individuals! Without us they don't have a platofrm to work from. All of these content creators go out of their way to promote leftist ideas and help change the dynamic of our current political system. You will find a link to each creators patreon if availaible to donate or join.
